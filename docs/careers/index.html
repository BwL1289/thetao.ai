What is Pluvio?

At the heart of Pluvio is a passionate group of people who love to make awesome products that delight people. We’re working to help people feel understood online by creating powerful, artificial intelligence powered software to bring in-store experiences to the web.

How We Work

  • We believe passionate people don’t need to be micro-managed.
  • You will not be told to work "startup" hours. You're expected to work 40 hours per week during business hours Eastern Time.
  • You can work from home, from a co-working space or from your favorite park. We are a truly remote-first company.
  • We follow the engineering process used in leading tech companies.
  • We believe in the power of collaboration and learning from each other, so expect to have many pair-coding and brainstorming sessions.
  • We are interested in nailing deadlines, not in your daily or hourly productivity.

What are our Core Values?

  • Be Transparent
  • Be Proud of Your Work
  • Aim for Perfection but Understand Reality
  • Lose the Ego
  • Always be Coaching
  • Be Constantly Curious
